The Hidden Costs of the Laundromat

If you're like many South Mississippi residents, you might be making regular trips to the laundromat. While it seems convenient, those costs add up quickly.

Average Laundromat Costs

  • Washing: $3-5 per load
  • Drying: $2-4 per load
  • Average family: 4-6 loads per week
  • Monthly total: $80-180+

And that doesn't include the hidden costs:

  • Gas to drive there and back
  • Time spent waiting (2-3 hours per trip)
  • Wear and tear on your clothes from commercial machines

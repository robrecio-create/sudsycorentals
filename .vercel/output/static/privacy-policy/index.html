Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 25, 2026

Who We Are

Sudsy Co. Rentals ("we," "our," or "us") operates the website sudsycorentals.com and provides washer and dryer rental services on the Mississippi Gulf Coast, including Gulfport, Biloxi, Ocean Springs, D'Iberville, Long Beach, Gautier, Pascagoula, Pass Christian, and Moss Point.

Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of personal information:

  • Contact information: Name, email address, phone number, and mailing address when you submit a contact form, schedule a delivery, or sign up for our rental service.
  • Payment information: Billing details processed securely through our third-party payment processor (Stripe). We do not store your credit card numbers on our servers.
  • Usage data: Information about how you interact with our website, including pages visited, time spent, and referring URLs, collected through Google Analytics and Meta Pixel.
  • Device information: Browser type, operating system, and IP address for security and analytics purposes.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Process and fulfill your washer and dryer rental orders
  • Schedule deliveries, installations, and maintenance visits
  • Communicate with you about your account, orders, and service updates
  • Send promotional offers and newsletters (with your consent)
  • Improve our website, services, and customer experience
  • Comply with legal obligations and prevent fraud

Cookies and Tracking

Our website uses cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance your browsing experience and analyze site traffic. These include:

  • Google Analytics (G-Q1Y72LG58V): Helps us understand website usage patterns and improve our content.
  • Google Ads (AW-17921626656): Used for conversion tracking and remarketing to show relevant ads.
  • Meta Pixel: Helps us measure the effectiveness of our Facebook and Instagram advertising.

You can control cookie preferences through your browser settings. Disabling cookies may affect some website functionality.

How We Share Your Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share your data with trusted third parties solely to operate our business:

  • Payment processors (Stripe) to handle transactions securely
  • Analytics providers (Google, Meta) to measure site performance
  • Communication tools to send order confirmations and service notifications

Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. Payment data is encrypted and processed through PCI-compliant services.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access, correct, or delete your personal information
  • Opt out of marketing communications at any time
  • Request a copy of the data we hold about you
  • Withdraw consent for data processing where applicable

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at (228) 338-3455 or through our website contact form.

Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to individuals under 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date. We encourage you to review this page periodically.
